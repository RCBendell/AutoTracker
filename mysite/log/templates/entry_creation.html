Create Log Entry

Log Entries can be used to track work done to your vehicle, interesting developments, or even just the mileage of your vehicle

For example, create one evertime you change the oil, everytime you wash it to record the cost, or occasionally just to track the mileage increase of your vehicle.


Associate the Entry with one of your vehicles.

Write a message that details what you did to your vehicle, or what you had done.

If it cost you anything, add the price in the 'Cost' field.

Set the date associated with the information in the message.

REQUIRED: Use the mileage to update the recorded mileage on your vehicle to track it.


NOTE: Each Entry can have one File and one Image associated with it to assist in your tracking which can be added after creation by Updating the Entry
