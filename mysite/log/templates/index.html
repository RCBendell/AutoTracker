Garage Manager

Welcome to Garage Manager, a web app created by Robert C. Bendell!


About

The Garage Manager was created to assist the everyday vehicle owner in tracking their personal vehicle maintenance.
Growing up in a family that enjoyed older vehicles, I was subject to several common mechanic tasks in order to maintain our vehicles.
Historically, car owners have kept paper records and journals to track maintenance and spending throughout their vehicles lifespan, but that procedure has become outdated and honestly messy.
This application is meant to be a place to track and maintain a history of someone's vehicles by providing an online journaling system that can be customized for each and every car in your fleet.
Using this application one can maintain a record of each vehicle, in a single place, including generic statistics like mileage and oil type as well as more tedious information like being a place to store receipts, and to track parts with included warranties.


How to Use

  1. Create an Account
  2. Confirm Your Account Using the link sent to your provided Email
  3. Navigate to the Login page, and sign in to your new account
  4. Populate your Garage with the Vehicles you own, or would like to track the maintenance for
  5. Begin Creating Entries everytime you:
    1. Perform Mainenance
    2. Upgrade Parts
    3. Change the Oil
    4. Have a Shop perform maintenance
    5. Change the color
    6. Want to Update the Mileage
    7. Or whatever else you do to your Vehicle!
  6. Continue doing so for your duration of ownsership to keep a full maintenance log for your vehicle!
